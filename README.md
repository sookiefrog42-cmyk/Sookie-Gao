This platform is dedicated to celebrate and amplify the voices of elderly women, whose life experiences, wisdom, and perspectives are often overlooked and underrepresented in mainstream society. 
The core mission of this website is to ensure that these voices are truly heard, valued, and preserved for future generations. 

We conduct in-depth interviews with senior women from diverse backgrounds, carefully documenting their personal journeys, life stories, struggles, and insights before compiling these narratives into written stories. 

Beyond oral storytelling preservation, the platform also collaborates with female craft creators to create a documentray about how embroidery artworks shapes their life, presenting women’s resilience and warmth in a tangible, artistic form. 

In addition to narrative collection and artistic creation, the website also supports in-depth academic and social research on housewives’ life experiences, unpaid domestic labor, and elderly women’s living conditions, aiming to raise public awareness, respect, and recognition for the lifelong contributions of ordinary elderly women and housewives.
